Pineapple Pizza

You probably thought that pineapple on pizza was bad, how about pizza on pineapple? Your taste buds will love it. It's a great dish for when you have somebody over or to surprise somoene that didn't know you were a master at cooking. Specially pizzas!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • 8 Roma tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 pineapple cut in half
  • 8 ounces shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • 4 ounces shredded Fontina cheese
  • 10 fresh basil leaves, washed, dried
  • ½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Stir together olive oil, garlic, and salt; toss with tomatoes, and allow to stand for 15 minutes. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  2. Brush each pineapple with some of the tomato marinade. Sprinkle the pineapple evenly with Mozzarella and Fontina cheeses. Arrange tomatoes overtop, then sprinkle with shredded basil, Parmesan, and feta cheese.

  3. Bake in preheated oven until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown, about 10 minutes.
